Our History

Marudhar Spinning Mills is a leading textile manufacturer that specializes in producing high-quality yarns for both domestic and international markets. Established in 1996, Marudhar Spinning Mills has been a pioneer in the textile industry, offering innovative solutions to meet the evolving needs of the market.


At Marudhar Spinning Mills, we are committed to sustainability and ethical practices. We use the latest technology and equipment to minimize our environmental footprint, and we ensure that our raw materials are sourced responsibly.
