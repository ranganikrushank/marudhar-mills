Welcome to Marudhar Mills

Marudhar Mills, established in 2024, is dedicated to providing premium yarns for the textile industry worldwide. Based in Surat, Gujarat, India, we specialize in producing high-quality yarns and collaborate with a network of skilled farmers to ensure our products meet global standards.

We, Marudhar Mills, are a leading Supplier and Exporter of high-quality yarns for the textile industry. Our company specializes in exporting Cotton Yarns, Blended Yarns, Synthetic Yarns, and Specialty Yarns. We are committed to delivering premium-quality products to our valued customers. With our skilled workforce, deep market knowledge, and dedication to excellence, we take pride in providing the best in customer service and sustainable textile solutions.
